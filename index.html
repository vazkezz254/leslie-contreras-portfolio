About me

I'm Leslie Lisuly Contreras Mendoza, a Master of Architecture student at Texas A&M University. My work explores the intersection of culture, community, and spatial experience. I'm passionate about designing spaces that are thoughtful, inclusive, and responsive to their environment. With each project, I aim to blend aesthetic clarity with purpose-driven function to create meaningful architectural solutions.
